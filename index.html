Leandro Niero (he/they) is a Brazilian/Italian developer, designer, and image maker based in London. They create web experiences and programme live visuals using a toolkit that includes full-stack development, generative AI, and art direction. Holding an MA in Computational Arts from Goldsmiths, University of London, Leandro has strengthened their commitment to merging research with practical application through the development of thought-provoking software with social impact. They are proficient in languages such as Java, C++, JavaScript, and Python, and have experience working with Agile methodology. Leandro’s portfolio includes personal projects as well as collaborations with musicians, brands, and non-profits. They are motivated by solving problems related to user experience and interaction, with a deep interest in promoting inclusivity and mental health through technology and innovative software solutions. Passionate about addressing social issues affecting the LGBTQ+ community, Leandro welcomes professional engagements and collaborative opportunities.
