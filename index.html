Leandro Niero (he/they) is a designer, developer and image maker based in London. They run Queer Horrors, direct music videos, and program live visuals using a toolkit that includes generative video and videography, creative coding, fabrication and art direction. In the web design sphere, Leandro focuses on responsive design and front-end development, employing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build user-centric digital experiences. Their portfolio includes collaborations with musicians, brands and non-profits, such as Alia, where Leandro has crafted digital platforms and safe spaces for community engagement and outreach. At Goldsmiths University of London, their MA in Computational Arts has fortified a commitment to melding research and practical application through development of thought provoking software that has social impact using languages like JavaScript, C++, and Python. Their time at Sandbox VR has also broaden their interest in VR/AR user experience design. Get in touch for professional engagements and collaborative prospects.
