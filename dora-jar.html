Dora Jar

In the creation of Dora Jar's music video, I played a role as an assistant to both the art director and the set builder. Collaborating closely under the guidance of production designer Dale Oliver Slater and director Jocelyn Anquetil, I contributed to the crafting of a visually enchanting setting that elevates the storytelling of the music. This project offered me the unique opportunity to immerse myself in the intricate processes of set design and artistic direction. My responsibilities encompassed aiding in the construction and arrangement of the set, ensuring each detail aligned seamlessly with the video's creative vision. The experience was both challenging and enriching, allowing me to witness firsthand the transformation of conceptual ideas into tangible, visually captivating elements. The music video, as a result, is not just a display of musical talent but also a testament to the collaborative art of storytelling through visual mediums.
