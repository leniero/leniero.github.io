piri & tommy

In the music video for piri & tommy, I had the opportunity to contribute as an assistant in both art direction and set building. Under the creative guidance of production designer Dale Oliver Slater and the direction of Rupert Höller, I played a supportive yet crucial role in bringing the visual narrative to life. My involvement in this project revolved around assisting in the meticulous planning and execution of the set design, a process that required a keen eye for detail and a hands-on approach. Working alongside seasoned professionals, I helped to materialise the artistic concepts into a physical setting that not only complements but also enhances the musical journey.
