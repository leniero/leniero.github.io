NFRH

In this ongoing collaboration with the collective 'Not From Round Here', I had the privilege of creating a series of visualisers, tailored specifically for each of the six DJs. These visualisers incorporate imagery from the DJs' home countries, seamlessly blended with motifs of spring and space. This design choice aligns with the thematic essence of the current parties, adding a unique and immersive layer to the live performances.
