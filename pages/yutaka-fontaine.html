Yutaka Fontaine

Music video created for Yutaka Fontaine’s track “Lucid Scream”. We aimed to capture the vibe of a dream you can't quite tell from reality. It's set to a progressive house track, with scenes flipping from the calm of dreamy parts to the energy of dance beats. We played with shadows and neon lights to set the mood and added a touch of mystery with two ninja-like figures representing the artists. It's a journey through the night, blending dreams with the rhythm of the city.
