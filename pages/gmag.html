This project employs a bespoke AI diffusion model, meticulously fine-tuned using a rich collection of imagery from the iconic Brazilian gay erotic publication 'G Magazine', a trailblazer in the 90s and early 00s. This initiative seeks to bridge historical queer aesthetics with the cutting-edge realm of generative art, catapulting the distinct visual narratives of a bygone era into today's digital forefront. By integrating queer Latin cultural elements into the fabric of Stable Diffusion models, the aim is to rejuvenate and preserve a pivotal piece of queer history, ensuring its resonance with contemporary and future audiences. This fusion of technology and art not only pays homage to a seminal publication but also paves the way for greater representation and inclusivity in AI-driven artistic endeavours. The GMAG diffusion model is available on Civitai.
