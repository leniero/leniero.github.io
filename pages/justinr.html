Justin Renaissance

In a collaboration with San Diego-based singer Justin Renaissance, I had the privilege of creating a series of audio reactive artworks that formed the visual backbone of his live performance. This project was an opportunity to blend my skills in computational arts with the dynamic world of music, resulting in a captivating live experience. At the heart of this project was the challenge of developing visuals that not only complemented Justin Renaissance's musical style but also responded in real-time to the sonic elements of his performance. This required a deep understanding of both the musical content and the technical intricacies involved in creating audio reactive art.
