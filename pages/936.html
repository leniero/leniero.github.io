NueveTresSeis

Cover art design for NueveTresSeis – "No me distraigas". Capturing the essence of introspection through a long-exposure portrait that radiates with internal conflict and clarity. A striking red glow from the subject's eye pierces the image, symbolising the intensity of personal revelation and the heat of emotions contained within. This image serves as a powerful visual for the song's exploration of inner truth and consciousness.
