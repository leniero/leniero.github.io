Aitor Throup

This book design proposal for Aitor Throup is inspired by his unique approach to fashion and design. I started by gathering all the images and illustrations he has created over the years. Using these visuals, I developed layout ideas that reflect the style and themes of his work. The design is influenced by Throup's fashion pieces, with layouts that highlight the details and creativity of his designs. The goal is to create a book that not only showcases his work but also captures the innovative and artistic style that defines Aitor Throup.
